About

18 y/o Nerd from Giza, Egypt

Hi, I’m Hazem Moghni, an 18 y/o graduate from STEM October, a top Egyptian public high school. I’m currently creating videos on YouTube.

Regarding academics, I am interested in physics (esp. astronomy), math (esp. statistics), and philosophy (esp. ethics).

In my leisure time, I enjoy reading DC comics, analyzing data I find in the wild (see my Friday Blog!), and playing chess.

STEM October Graduate

High school was exciting. I co-led three school clubs (physics, math, and English) while mentoring students in another two (research and astronomy).

I started some iniatitives like the International Physics Realm Competition and the Egypt2USA Discord server (online hub for college applications). Finally, I co-founded a chess club for fun.

Perhaps most importantly, I made amazing friends that, at times of stress, made everything better — psst, you can see them on my YouTube Channel.

Aspiring Engineer & Scientist

Career-wise, I’d like to pursue either mechanical engineering or physics.
